Depression symptoms can vary from mild to severe and can include:

  • Feeling sad or having depressed mood
  • Loss of interest or pleassure in activities once enjoyed
  • Changes in appetite - weight loss or gain unrelated to dieting
  • Trouble Sleeping or sleeping alot
  • Loss of energy/increased faitgue
  • Increase of purposeless physical activity(e.g, inability to sit still,pacing,hand writitng)or slowed movements or speech(these actions must be severe enough to be observable by others)
